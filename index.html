About Feed A Child

Learn about our mission, vision, and the work we do to fight child hunger

Who We Are

Our Mission

Feed A Child is a registered South African Public Benefit Organisation (PBO) dedicated to eradicating child hunger through sustainable solutions.

Our Vision

We envision a South Africa where no child goes to bed hungry, and every family has access to nutritious food.

What We Do

Nutrient-Rich Meals

We provide nutrient-rich instant porridge to children and families in need across South Africa.

Charity Shops

We operate charity shops in Centurion and Klerksdorp to fund our programs and support local communities.

Vegetable Gardens

We establish vegetable gardens for food security and teach communities how to grow their own food.

Entrepreneurial Training

We offer entrepreneurial training programs to help communities generate sustainable income.
